How it works

01
Study at your own pace

You decide when and where you study. NKI's online programs adapt to your schedule — not the other way around — so you can fit coursework around work, family, and everyday life.

02
Work through your digital classroom

Everything you need lives in one digital classroom: course materials and assignments, with the flexibility to submit work and book exams whenever you're ready.

03
Get support along the way

You're never studying alone. Message your teacher or study advisor directly, get a personal study advisor from day one, and connect with fellow students working through the same material.
